Landfall Island Wildlife Sanctuary, Andaman

Landfall Island Wildlife Sanctuary is nestled over the northernmost island of the Andaman archipelago and shelters a bizarre array of flora and fauna! It is one of the most beautiful animal sanctuaries in India with multiple ecosystems including clear blue lagoons, dense mangroves, golden beaches, tropical rainforests, wetlands, and backwaters. Home to the indigenous Aka-Chari tribe, this remote and untouched ecological hotspot spanning 30 sq km shelters peculiar wildlife like Andaman water monitors, hawksbill turtles, and green sea turtles.
